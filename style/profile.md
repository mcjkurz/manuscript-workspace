# Style profile

A compact, agent-maintained distillation of the references in `style/`.
The agent reads **this file**, not the full reference texts, when editing
prose. Rebuild it only when the references change or when the user asks.

## How to maintain

- Keep it short: a few paragraphs of notes plus a handful of representative
  sentences. Target well under one page; never paste whole articles here.
- Describe register, sentence length and rhythm, vocabulary and idiom,
  citation habits, paragraph shape, and any rhetorical moves to imitate or
  avoid.
- Quote sparingly and only to pin down a specific trait; attribute each
  quote to its source file.
- Regenerate from the originals in `style/` (and `style/processed/`) when
  references are added or changed. Sample the originals rather than
  loading them wholesale.
